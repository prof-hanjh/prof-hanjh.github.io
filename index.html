Profile

This website presents my academic profile, research areas, publications, students' theses, and research notes. My research focuses on global trade and supply chains, with particular attention to digital transformation, sustainability, retail and fashion supply chains, and cross-border e-commerce.

Research Areas

Digital Supply Chain

Digital transformation, supply chain analytics, AI-enabled supply chains, digital platforms, and supply chain resilience.

Retail & Fashion Supply Chain

Omni-channel integration, fashion retail, fast fashion, consumer behavior, and retail supply chain strategy.

Cross-border E-commerce

Digital trade, global platforms, international logistics, cross-border consumer experience, and e-commerce orchestration.

Sustainable Supply Chain

ESG implementation, green supply chain management, supply chain governance, interfirm relationships, and sustainable performance.
